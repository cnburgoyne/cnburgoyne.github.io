How to Watch Netflix

Specifically, how to find something to watch on Netflix if you have not decided on a movie or television show before opening Netflix.

  1. Open your favorite internet browser.
    2. Probably Google Chrome
  2. Go to nextflix.com.
  3. Sign in to your Netflix account.
    4. If you do not have one of your own, get login information from a friend.
  4. Browse available movies and television shows until you fall asleep.
    5. Notice: you never actually find anything good to watch before you fall asleep due to boredom.
